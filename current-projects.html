CURRENT PROJECTS

Workshops:
Microbit Magic with Zenius Labs
The BBC micro:bit is a tiny computer that makes learning to code simple and fun! In this ____ hour workshop, students will get to explore how software (the instructions you give your computer) and hardware (lights, buttons, sounds) mix together to make creative and functional projects.

Invent with Zenius Labs
Calling All Inventors! Have you ever wondered how things get invented? Get your imaginations and your pencil crayons ready as we dive into this two and a half hour workshop that uses creativity and STEAM to take you through the invention process. You will get to use your imagination to solve real-world problems with an invention of your very own. All while learning about how art meets science to solve problems in real life.

Play n Tell with Zenius Labs
We've all had the experience of buying a game or toy that we thought was going to be great and turns out to not work out the way we planned. Play n Tell with Zenius Labs lets you try all the up and coming STEAM toys before you buy them so you have a chance to try them out and make sure they're engaging and appropriate for your kids. We bring in a wide range of games and toys for kids at all ages and stages of STEAM exploration.
